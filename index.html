Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a UK visa application take?

Processing times vary by visa type. Standard visitor visas typically take 3 weeks, while work and family visas can take 3-8 weeks. We provide priority services for faster processing when available.

What documents do I need for my visa application?

Required documents depend on your visa type. Generally, you'll need a valid passport, financial evidence, accommodation proof, and supporting documents specific to your application. We provide a complete checklist during consultation.

Can you help if my visa was refused?

Yes, we specialize in immigration appeals and administrative reviews. We'll assess your refusal reasons and advise on the best course of action, whether reapplying or appealing the decision.

Do you offer payment plans?

Yes, we offer flexible payment plans for most immigration services. Contact us to discuss options that work for your budget while ensuring quality representation.
